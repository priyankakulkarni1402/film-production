How It All Began

MyFilmProduction was born in 2010 when two passionate filmmakers decided to bridge the gap between creative vision and commercial viability. What started as a small collective of storytellers has evolved into a full-service production company that consistently delivers extraordinary content for clients worldwide.

Our founders, having worked on various independent films and commercial projects, recognized the need for a production company that could combine artistic integrity with professional execution. They envisioned a place where creativity thrives, technical excellence is paramount, and every project is approached with fresh perspective and unwavering dedication.

Our Journey

Key milestones that have shaped MyFilmProduction into who we are today

2010 - The Beginning

Founded by two passionate filmmakers with a vision to create exceptional content. Started with a small team and big dreams.

2013 - First Major Campaign

Land our first major commercial campaign, establishing our reputation for excellence in advertising and brand storytelling.

2016 - Expansion

Expanded our team and services, adding post-production capabilities and motion graphics to our offerings.

2018 - Documentary Focus

Launched our documentary division, focusing on social impact films and meaningful storytelling projects.

2020 - Digital Innovation

Adapted to the digital landscape, creating virtual production capabilities and remote collaboration tools.

2024 - Today

Now a leading production company with over 50 team members, having completed 500+ projects across various industries.
