Our Post-Production Process

A systematic approach to delivering exceptional post-production results

01

Ingest & Organization

Organize all footage, audio files, and assets into a structured project folder with proper naming conventions and metadata

02

Offline Edit

Create the rough cut sequence, selecting the best shots, timing the scenes, and establishing the narrative flow

03

Online Edit

Refine the edit with the original high-resolution footage, add transitions, titles, and basic effects

04

Color Grading

Apply color correction and creative grading to achieve the desired visual mood and consistency

05

Audio Post

Clean up dialogue, add sound effects, music, and create a final mix that enhances the viewing experience

06

Final Delivery

Export and deliver the final content in the required formats, ready for broadcast or distribution
