Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive film production services including pre-production planning, principal photography, post-production editing, color grading, sound design, and final delivery. We also provide equipment rental and consultation services.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple commercial might take 2-3 weeks, while a complex documentary could take 2-3 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you work with businesses of all sizes?

Absolutely! We work with startups, small businesses, and large corporations. Our team adapts to your needs and budget to deliver quality content that achieves your goals.

What's included in your production packages?

Our packages typically include pre-production planning, professional crew, equipment rental, shooting, basic editing, color correction, and final delivery. Custom packages are available based on your specific needs.
