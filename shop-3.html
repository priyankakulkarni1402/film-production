Audio Quality Standards

Understanding the technical specifications that ensure professional audio quality

Sample Rate

Broadcast Standard: 48 kHz
High Quality: 96 kHz
Music Production: 192 kHz

Higher sample rates capture more detail but require more storage and processing power

Bit Depth

Standard: 16-bit
Professional: 24-bit
Latest Technology: 32-bit float

Higher bit depth provides greater dynamic range and more detailed audio representation

Frequency Response

Human Hearing: 20Hz - 20kHz
Dialogue Focus: 80Hz - 8kHz
Full Range: 20Hz - 20kHz

Microphones and equipment should cover the full human hearing range for complete audio capture

Signal-to-Noise Ratio

Good: 70-80 dB
Professional: 80-90 dB
Premium: 90+ dB

Higher signal-to-noise ratio means cleaner audio with less background noise and hiss
